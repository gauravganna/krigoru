Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Thank you for ordering from Krigoru. Below is our shipping policy for all journal orders placed through Instagram or WhatsApp.

1. Order Placement

Orders are placed directly through our Instagram page (@krigoru) or via WhatsApp. Once you confirm your order and complete payment through Razorpay, we will begin processing your order.

2. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation. You will receive a confirmation message on Instagram or WhatsApp once your order has been dispatched.

3. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship across India. All deliveries are handled through trusted courier partners to ensure your journals reach you safely.

4. Delivery Time

  • Metro cities: 3–5 business days after dispatch.
  • Other cities and towns: 5–7 business days after dispatch.
  • Remote areas: 7–10 business days after dispatch.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and may vary due to courier service conditions, weather, or other unforeseen circumstances.

5. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be communicated to you at the time of order confirmation. We may offer free shipping on select orders or during promotional periods.

6. Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, we will share the tracking details with you via Instagram DM or WhatsApp so you can track your delivery in real time.

7. Incorrect Address

Please ensure that the shipping address you provide is accurate and complete. Krigoru is not responsible for delays or non-delivery caused by incorrect or incomplete address information.

8. Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us within 48 hours of the expected delivery date. We will work with our courier partner to resolve the issue and arrange a replacement or refund as appropriate.

9. Contact Us

For any shipping-related queries, please reach out to us:

  • Email: hello@krigoru.com
  • Instagram: @krigoru
  • WhatsApp: Message us
