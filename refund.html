Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

At Krigoru, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please read our cancellation and refund policy below.

1. Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order under the following conditions:

  • Before dispatch: If your order has not yet been dispatched, you can request a cancellation by contacting us via Instagram DM, WhatsApp, or email. A full refund will be issued.
  • After dispatch: Once your order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You may, however, request a return after delivery (see below).

2. Returns

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery under the following conditions:

  • The journal must be unused and in its original packaging.
  • The product must not be damaged, written in, or altered in any way.
  • Personalised or custom-made journals are not eligible for return.

To initiate a return, contact us with your order details and reason for return. We will provide instructions for shipping the product back.

3. Refund Process

  • Once we receive and inspect the returned product, we will notify you of the refund status.
  • If approved, the refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method via Razorpay.
  • Shipping charges (if any were paid) are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect product.

4. Defective or Incorrect Products

If you receive a defective or incorrect product:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the issue.
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund (including shipping charges) at no extra cost to you.

5. Damaged During Shipping

If your journal arrives damaged due to shipping, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We will work with our courier partner to resolve the issue and arrange a replacement or refund.

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refund or return:

  • Journals that have been used, written in, or damaged by the customer.
  • Products returned after the 7-day return window.
  • Personalised or custom-made journals.

7. How to Contact Us

For cancellations, returns, or refund enquiries, please reach out to us:

  • Email: hello@krigoru.com
  • Instagram: @krigoru
  • WhatsApp: Message us
